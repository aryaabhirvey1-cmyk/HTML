TOP ANIMES TO BINGE WATCH !!

Shingeki no kyogin:

Set in a world where humanity lives within enormous walled cities to protect themselves from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans, Attack on Titan is a story of survival, freedom, and vengeance. The series follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight the Titans after their hometown is destroyed. The anime is known for its deep political themes, complex characters, and shocking twists that question the very nature of humanity and power. The story begins with a shocking event — a colossal Titan breaches Wall Maria, allowing smaller Titans to invade and destroy Eren Yeager’s hometown. Witnessing his mother’s death, Eren vows to annihilate every Titan. Along with his childhood friends, Mikasa Ackerman and Armin Arlert, he joins the military’s Survey Corps, a special branch that ventures outside the walls to fight Titans and uncover their mysteries.

One Piece

. Luffy, who gained rubber-like powers after eating a Devil Fruit, sets sail with his diverse crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, on an epic adventure across a fantasy world of oceans and islands to find the treasure and fulfill his dream. The story is a grand adventure that explores themes of achieving dreams and the bonds of found family. A live-action adaptation also premiered on Netflix in 2023, which received positive reviews and has been renewed for subsequent seasons.
